Intent

My intent with this project is to display the states of matter (gas/liquid/solid) in a visual way by bringing the words to life using animation. I used pwbubbles from dafont for gas because it was bubbly and bubbles are made from air/gas. I wanted to display a feeling of freedom by letting the bubbles rise from the word gas. For liquid I used a variable font called recursive. For this word composition I wanted to display fluidity and how the liquid matter takes many forms of shape. For solid I used the font Plaster from google fonts. It is a very geometric font that shows a lot of shape. I wanted to display how solid objects portray strength. It is different from a liquid and gas because they are stable and held in place. I displayed this aspect of solid objects by letting each block of shape in the font bump into each other showing that they can collide with each other while keeping their original structure.
